隱私權政策

我們尊重您的隱私，並致力於保護您在使用 TRIANGLE COLOR 服務時所提供的個人資料。

  • 資料用途：僅用於預約、交付、售後與品牌溝通，不會外洩或販售。
  • 儲存安全：所有資料皆採加密儲存，並定期進行安全稽核。
  • 查詢與刪除：您可隨時聯絡我們查詢或要求刪除個人資料。
  • 第三方服務：若需與物流、支付或合作夥伴共享資料，僅限於完成交易或服務所必須，並確保其遵守同等隱私標準。
  • Cookie 與追蹤：本網站可能使用 Cookie 以改善瀏覽體驗，但不會用於跨站追蹤或廣告販售。
  • 未成年人保護：我們不會主動收集 18 歲以下使用者的個人資料，若發現將立即刪除。
  • 國際合規：我們遵循 GDPR、CCPA 等國際隱私法規，確保跨境資料處理的合法性。
  • 透明度報告：若有政府或法律單位要求資料，我們將於符合法律的前提下，盡可能通知用戶。
  • 資料保存期限：個人資料僅於必要期間內保存，超過期限將安全刪除或匿名化。
  • 使用者權利：您有權要求更正資料、限制處理，或拒絕特定用途。
  • 政策更新：若有隱私政策更新，將於網站公告並即時生效。

Privacy Policy

We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting the personal data you provide when using TRIANGLE COLOR services.

  • Data Usage: Used only for reservations, delivery, after-sales, and brand communication. Never disclosed or sold.
  • Storage Security: All data is encrypted and subject to regular security audits.
  • Access & Deletion: You may contact us anytime to request access to or deletion of your data.
  • Third-Party Services: Data may be shared with logistics, payment, or partners only when necessary to complete a transaction, under strict privacy standards.
  • Cookies & Tracking: Cookies may be used to improve browsing experience, but never for cross-site tracking or ad sales.
  • Protection of Minors: We do not knowingly collect data from users under 18. Any such data will be promptly deleted.
  • International Compliance: We comply with GDPR, CCPA, and other global privacy regulations to ensure lawful cross-border data handling.
  • Transparency Reports: If data is requested by government or legal authorities, we will notify users whenever legally permissible.
  • Data Retention: Personal data is retained only as long as necessary, after which it is securely deleted or anonymized.
  • User Rights: You have the right to request corrections, restrict processing, or object to certain uses of your data.
  • Policy Updates: Any updates to this policy will be announced on our website and take immediate effect.
